All Bourbon is Whiskey.

But not all Whiskey is Bourbon!

To become Bourbon, the Whiskey must be:

____________________

1. Distilled in the United States of America

2. Made with a mashbill of at least 51% corn

3. Aged in new, charred oak containers

4. Distilled no higher than 160 proof

5. Enter the barrel no higher than 125 proof

6. Bottled no lower than 80 proof

95% of all bourbon comes from Kentucky.

`

Bourbon is a $8.5 billion industry in Kentucky.

`

Over 1,800,000 barrels were filled in Kentucky last year.

`

There are now over 6,600,000 barrels aging in Kentucky.
